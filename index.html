N2O Protocols

Introduction

N2O Protocols for commercial use on top of the MQTT protocol. Provide an EMQ extension that immediately activates the N2O protocol and its applications for all connected MQTT devices. Create a single Erlang eco-system for Enterprise Protocol Federation and establish a solid CORBA/WS-SOAP/XMPP-replacement, ready for high-speed, low-latency IoT applications.
